The iCTF is a global hacking contest organized by UCSB and Shellphish, in which hundreds of teams compete to hack into each other's machines and steal precious flags, while simultaneously defending their own machine from attackers.

Have you ever wondered how a world without memory corruption would look like?
In this 2021 edition of iCTF, "Hodl!", players will be challenged to hack their way through the most secure challenges in this memory-corruption-free attack-defense competition. The format is simple: Attack other teams, take their flags, fix the bugs, and score points!
With that said, your languages might be safe, but be cautious out there! Go and get those flags!
